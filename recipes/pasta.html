Pasta e fagioli

This is a famous recipe originally from Italy and now can be found in many cuisines across Asia, Europe and even Africa and the rest of the world! Today ,I bring this recipe to you!

Ingredients

These are the required ingredients for making pasta meal

  1. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  2. 1 medium onion, chopped
  3. 2 stalks celery, chopped
  4. 3 cloves garlic minced
  5. 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  6. 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  7. salt to taste
  8. 1(14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
  9. 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  10. 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  11. 1/2cup ditalini and other small pasta
  12. 1(15 ounce) can cannellini beans, with liquid

Steps

  • Make the dough
  • knead the dough
  • Roll out the dough
  • Cut the pasta
  • Cook the pasta
